Project Storage Rules and Regulations

Welcome to our project storage platform! To ensure a smooth and efficient experience for all users, please adhere to the following rules and guidelines when uploading your projects:

Upload Format Guidelines

  • Your project files should be compressed into a single ZIP archive.
  • Include a clear and informative project title within the ZIP archive.
  • Provide a concise project description or abstract.
  • Include all necessary project files, documents, and resources within the ZIP archive.

Plagiarism Policy

We have a strict plagiarism policy to ensure the originality of projects:

  • All projects must be the original work of the uploader.
  • Plagiarism is not allowed, and projects should have less than 20% similarity with existing projects.
  • Cite and credit any external sources, references, or inspiration used in your project.

Reliability and Efficiency

To maintain a reliable and efficient platform:

  • Ensure that your project files are complete and functional.
  • Avoid uploading projects with similarities to existing ones to reduce confusion.


Failure to comply with these rules may result in the removal of your project from our platform. Thank you for your cooperation.
